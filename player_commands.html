Aion 3.9: Ethereal Aion - Player Commands!

.toll value <Toll Amount>

Exhanges kinah into tolls, 1 toll = 100k kinah

.dropinfo

Lists the items you may loot from the target

.relics

Exchanges every relic in your inventory

.medal

Exchanges 3 platinum medals into 3 gold medal

.dual <attack|crit|magical>

Exchanges 40 mithril medals into a dual-stats manastone bundle

.morph

Cancels your transformations without removing the buff

.recharger

Teleports you to the duel area

.dye <color|hexcode>

Dyes all dyable parts (costs 5m kinah)

.queue

Registers you for the ongoing event

VIP Commands

.preview <id>

Previews any skin on you for 10 seconds

.remodel <id>

Remodels the appropriate item, requires a remodel item

.augment

Augments your equipment, permanently useable and free

.rgb <color> <message>

Allows you to type a message in the specified color

In case you may have some issues:

.gmlist

Shows the GM's available

.delete_item

Tag an item from your inventory to delete it

.mcheck

Reloads all quests

.skills

Reloads all skills

.asmo

Reloads all stigmaslots and teleports for asmo

.ely

Reloads all stigmaslots and teleports for ely
